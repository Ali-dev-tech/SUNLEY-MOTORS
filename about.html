About Sunley Motors

Your Trusted Partner in Auto Spare Parts Since 1990

Our Story

Founded in 1990, Sunley Motors has been at the forefront of the automotive spare parts industry for over three decades. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in auto parts supply, serving customers across the region with unwavering commitment to quality and service.

Our journey has been driven by a simple yet powerful mission: to provide high-quality auto parts at competitive prices while delivering exceptional customer service. This commitment has helped us build lasting relationships with our customers, from individual car owners to major automotive service centers.
